Lexium AI – Intelligent Educational Assistant
My role. Full-Stack AI Developer

Project description.

Lexium AI is an AI-powered educational assistant built with RAG to transform textbooks, lecture notes, and course materials into an interactive tutor. Students can ask natural-language questions and receive accurate, context-aware answers instantly. Piloted with 2,000+ FAST NUCES students, it boosted engagement and reduced repetitive faculty workload by enabling personalized, self-paced learning.
